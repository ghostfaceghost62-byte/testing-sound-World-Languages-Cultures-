About France

France is a country in Western Europe known for its history, art, cuisine, and romantic landscapes. From the Eiffel Tower and Louvre in Paris to the lavender fields of Provence and the Riviera beaches, France is a diverse and fascinating country. French culture is renowned for fashion, wine, gastronomy, art, and literature. Visitors enjoy exploring historic cities, enjoying gourmet food, visiting world-famous museums, and taking part in regional festivals. The country has a rich heritage with medieval towns, châteaux, and scenic countryside.



Foods


  • Baguette

    The baguette is a long, thin loaf of French bread known for its crisp crust and soft interior. It is an iconic symbol of French cuisine.


    Recipe

    • 500g all-purpose flour
    • 300ml water
    • 10g salt
    • 7g dry yeast

    Instructions: Mix ingredients, knead dough, let rise, shape into baguette, let rise again, and bake at 220°C for 25-30 minutes.

Tourist Destination


1. Eiffel Tower, Paris





The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark in Paris and a symbol of France. Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the city from its observation decks.


2. Mont Saint-Michel, Normandy





Mont Saint-Michel is a historic island commune in Normandy, famous for its medieval abbey, tidal island, and picturesque views. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Languages


The official language of France is French. It is spoken throughout the country and is known for its elegance and global influence.
